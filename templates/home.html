Workflow

Pick the search area with a smartphone, Needlee will start an autonomous flight.

Watch live stream video while Needlee scans the area to detect people.

Recieve HD images of search targets and send team to validated GPS coordinates.

Use cases

Needlee is focused on a single task to solve it perfectly: searching and detecting people. This approach allows the drone to distribute its resources efficiently, outperforming multi-mission UAVs.

An ideal combination of hardware and software for human detection leads a fundamentally new level of search operations such as:

Ground rescue missions

Maritime observing

Border guarding
