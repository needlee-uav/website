Needlee AI: smart search operations

More than 90% of search and rescue teams use first-person-view drones in their operations. Finding people from a bird's eye view with the human eye is almost impossible.

A fully autonomous Needlee Drone solves this problem: AI will take over the recognition and validation of people in difficult terrain, reporting detected targets to your web application.

Workflow

Needlee complex includes an AI drone (or a group of drones) and the Needlee Console: a flight control web application.

All the flight data comes to the Console, so even multiple drones could be managed easily with a single operator.

Pick a search area with your smartphone, Needlee will start an autonomous flight.

Watch live stream video while Needlee scans the area to detect people.

Recieve HD images of search targets and send team to validated GPS coordinates.

Use cases

Needlee is focused on a single task to solve it perfectly: searching and detecting people. This approach allows the drone to distribute its resources efficiently, outperforming multi-mission UAVs.

An ideal combination of hardware and software for human detection leads a fundamentally new level of search operations such as:

Search & rescue ground missions

Maritime observing

Border guarding
